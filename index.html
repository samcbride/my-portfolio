About Me

Hello! My name is Shona McBride and I'm originally from Vancouver, Canada. I moved to Manchester with my boyfriend almost one year ago after spending a year in Barcelona. My parents live in the east of Canada and I have two sisters and two nieces all of whom lived in Barcelona until earlier this year when one of my sisters recently moved to the Netherlands.  I grew up in North Vancouver, a suburb of Vancouver, which is close to the mountains. I originally trained as a nurse in Vancouver and worked in the profession for four years before deciding to move to Barcelona to be closer to family for a while.

Some of the hobbies I enjoy are:

  • Bouldering
  • Rowing
  • Hiking
  • Being outdoors
  • Reading
  • Occasionally sewing clothes and then immediately hating them for some reason..

I decided to try a web development bootcamp after being introduced to the world of coding through my boyfriend. I'd never thought I could do anything involving tech, but after trying out some free online resources I found I really quite liked it. I'm excited for all the learning that's coming up and to look back on the progress I've made at the end.
